About Emotional Sage

"We strive to understand and help deliver the most effective text based customer service experience by allowing customer service agents to see the emotion of the client in real-time text conversations.""

- George J. Conidis
(Developer and Founder of Emotional Sage)

The gap between customer service agents and their clients has been diminished in a whole new way. Emotional Sage ensures customer service agents provide clients with a fullfilling and engaging experience through an understanding of what emotional state they are in from their texts and identify churn vulnerability. Emotional Sage elevates the client experience to a whole new level.
Contact the Emotional Sage Team to get your Customer Support Team equiped with the emotional neeeds of your clients. !
